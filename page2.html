COFFEE WITH OWN STORY

OUR STORY

Through history, cafes have been a place to meet friends, work, and provide a source of inspiration. I'm a half filipino and half spanish so I decided to formulate menu that merge those things. Our cafe is inspired by fushion of cultures. There are also lot of extensive breakfast,lunch and dinner menus with a creative offerings.We also offer events like poetry readings that would make the customer feel more better and comfortable. The events in my life inspired me to take plunge and follow my dream. Those events makes me realize that "If you want something, do it now". I decided to name my establishment as CHES CAFE after my second name which is Cheska. Some of my menu are inspired by my mother's recipe which she taught me a lot of things in terms of informal training, culinary arts. This past July 2018, we celebrated our first anniversary.

DISCOVER

Take a break in our cafe. Our beautiful new cafe serves a range of hot and cold drinks, sandwiches, salads, snacks, ice creams and freshly baked cakes. If you have any allergies or special dietary requirements, please speak to a member of cafe staff who will do their best to accommodate you. You are welcome to bring your own food and drink into Discover. however we ask that cafe seating is reserved for customers who have bought food and drink from the cafe. You'll find the cafe near the town plaza of San Fenando La Union. We can also provide you different kind of books that you will surely love. Our cafe is open from 8:00 in the morning until 8:00 pm during weekdays while during weekends we are open 9:00 am until 10:00 in the evening.
